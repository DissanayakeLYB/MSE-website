Society of Materials Engineering Students (SOMES)

Fostering innovation and collaboration in materials science for a sustainable future.

About SOMES

SOMES is a vibrant community of students dedicated to exploring the exciting world of materials science. Through workshops, projects, and events, we aim to inspire and equip the next generation of engineers and scientists.

  • Hands-on learning opportunities
  • Networking with industry professionals
  • Promoting sustainable practices

Our Activities

Engaging events and initiatives to drive innovation and collaboration.

Materials Innovation Workshop

A hands-on workshop exploring cutting-edge materials and their applications.

Annual Research Symposium

Showcasing innovative research by students and faculty members.

Community Outreach Programs

Promoting awareness of materials science through community engagement.
