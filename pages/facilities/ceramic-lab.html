Ceramic Laboratory

Our Ceramic Lab is dedicated to the development and analysis of advanced ceramic materials for various applications.

Equipped with cutting-edge tools, we focus on innovation in ceramics for structural, electronic, and biomedical uses.

Lab Features

Material Synthesis

Advanced equipment for synthesizing high-performance ceramic materials.

Shaping & Sintering

Facilities for shaping and sintering ceramic components with precision.

Thermal Analysis

State-of-the-art tools for analyzing thermal properties of ceramics.

Ongoing Research

Structural Ceramics
