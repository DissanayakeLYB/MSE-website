Heat Treatment Laboratory

Our lab is equipped with cutting-edge tools and technologies, enabling advanced research and practical applications in the field of heat treatment.

From material strengthening to microstructural analysis, we provide an environment for academic excellence and industrial collaboration.

Lab Features

High-Temperature Furnaces

State-of-the-art furnaces for annealing, tempering, and hardening processes.

Quenching Systems

Advanced quenching equipment for controlled cooling experiments.

Microstructure Analysis

Precision tools for examining material transformations post-treatment.
